Maptime.ME is a monthly map hack night, alternating between Portland and Bangor.

We tell stories with maps, investigate interesting geospatial datasets, and learn about the latest cartographic tools.

If you'd like to keep up to date on upcoming events, join our Meetup Group. Alternatively, we have a twitter account over at @MaptimeME. We look forward to hearing from you!

Pull requests are welcome; our Github repository lives over at jacquestardie/maptime.

If you have any questions/comments, send an email to Jacques.
